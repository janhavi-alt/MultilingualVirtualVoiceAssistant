About Edugenie

Edugenie is a real-time virtual multilingual voice assistant that helps students learn in Hindi, Marathi, and English.

Key Features

  • 🎙 Voice-Based Learning in Hindi, Marathi, and English
  • 📐 Math Solver with Step-by-Step Explanations
  • 🔬 Physics & Chemistry Problem Solving
  • 🧬 Biology Concept Explanations

Future Plans

Upcoming features include AI-driven personalized learning, Raspberry Pi integration, and Telegram bot support.
